ABOUT US

WE Team Creators_Squad comes up with an efficient solution "SENTYECTOR" to make life wonderful for each and every individual reducing all kind of communication gap. With the help of the sentyector one can easily analysize and look after how a person needs to be handled and what kind of speech one can use. The application is useful for quickly gaining insights using large volumes of data.





Reducing Generation Gap:The generation gap can easily be overcome through the detector. Youngsters and elders can easily analyize their speeches in order to understand each other's perspective.

Relieving mental stress : With the stressful person one can easily use the detector so that his sentiments in the speech should be recorded easily and moreover one could communicate easily

Women Empowerment : The detector can be installed in the public places where it could detect the abusive languages and can easily alarm and make people aware of child and women abuse around themselves

Emotion Detection : This aims to detect emotions, like happiness, frustration, anger, sadness, and so on. It can detect the whether the information is educative, inspiration, motivational, sarcastic
